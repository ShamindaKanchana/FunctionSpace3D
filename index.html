Equation syntax help
  • Power: x^2, (x+y)^3
  • Multiply: * (use it between variables/numbers), e.g. 2*x or x*y
  • Divide: /, e.g. (x^2+y^2)/4
  • Add/subtract: +, -
  • Functions: sin(), cos(), tan(), exp(), log(), sqrt(), abs()
  • Surfaces: z = ...; curves: y = ... or x = ...; implicit: x^2+y^2+z^2=4
  • Animation: use t in expressions, e.g. z = sin(x+t)*cos(y)
